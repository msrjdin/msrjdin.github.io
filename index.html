Academic Projects

Diagnosis of Heart Disease by integrating Naïve Bayes with K-Means Clustering
This project predicts the probability of Heart Stroke based on the input parameters given. The project, basically, is divided into two parts Clustering and Classification. The algorithm is trained with training data to divide it into K clusters and then classify it to diagnose the heart disease patients.

Creating a Twitter Dataset excluding bots contribution
I was selected for this project of TEQIP phase-II worth Rs 1 Lakh. In this modern era of bots, flooding social media and manipulating people has become far more easier than anything. This project collects a considerable amount of tweets for a given trend or hashtag and filters it by removing the tweets contributed by bots. Firstly, individual user tweets are examined and many factors are considered, like the negativity or positivity of the tweets, number of tweets per period, timing of the tweet etc, which determines it whether it is a human or bot.
